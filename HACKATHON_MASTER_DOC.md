### The "Silent Pandemic" in India
In India, a scam occurs every **few seconds**. It‚Äôs not just an "IT problem"‚Äîit‚Äôs a social crisis.

- **The UPI Trap**: A student trying to sell an old phone on OLX is sent a "Receive QR Code" that actually drains their bank account.
- **The KYC Panic**: A retired pensioner gets an SMS saying their "Electricity will be cut tonight" unless they click a link. They panic, they click, and their life savings vanish.
- **The Courier Ransom**: A housewife is told a parcel in her name contains illegal items and she must pay "police fees" over a video call to avoid arrest.
- **The Job Mirage**: An unemployed graduate is promised a job at a big MNC but is asked for a "security deposit" first.

### Why current tools fail:
1. **Truecaller is reactive**: It only flags numbers *after* thousands are already scammed. It doesn't read the message content.

3. **Google Filters are slow**: Most Indian scans use **Hinglish** or localized images which standard global filters miss.

**Current tools tell you WHO is calling. ScamShield tells you WHAT they are trying to do to you.**
